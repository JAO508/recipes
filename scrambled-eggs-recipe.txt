Ingredients:
  -eggs
  -milk or sparkling water
  -butter
  -pepper
  -salt
  -chives as you like

Directions:

  1. Mix the eggs and the milk/sparkling water together. Use two tablespoons of
    milk/water for one egg. Then add the chives and mix it again.

  2. Give the butter into the pan and heat it to maximum temperature.

  3. When the butter is fully melted give the egg mixture in the pan and use a
    spatula to scratch on the bottom of the pan. Turn the heat down so the egg
    will not burn, but just stumble.
    
  4. When egg is fully stumbled, it is ready to eat.
